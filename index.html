How it works
Step 1
Students donate
Any student with unused meal swipes can contribute any amount — even just one. Donations are anonymous.
Step 2
Requests are reviewed
Students in need submit a confidential request. Campus Dean of Students offices review and approve within 24 hours.
Step 3
Swipes are transferred
Approved swipes are loaded directly to the student's campus dining account — no stigma, no in-person pickup required.
About this initiative

Swipe Solidarity was proposed to the UNC Association of Student Governments (UNCASG) as a system-wide expansion of meal swipe donation pilots already running at individual campuses. Research shows that over 30% of UNC students experience food insecurity at some point during their studies. This portal serves as the unified infrastructure to make peer-to-peer food support available across all 17 institutions — regardless of whether a student's home campus has its own program.

This initiative was developed as part of NCSSM's Director of State Relations proposal for the 2026–2027 academic year.
